1D Cellular Automation

A set of rules for 1d cellular automation were developed by mathematician Stephen Wolfram. They specify rules for generating new cells given a previous row generation of cells. It is amazing to see how mechanical rules operating on certain initial cell states can give rise to aestetic, and often lifelike animations.

Below are options to see some of them in action.

Press pause if you want to halt animations.

Press one of the rule buttons to resume the action. More on wolfram rules!
